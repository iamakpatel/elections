<H3> 2026 Candidates & Winning Chances</H3>
- Mamata Banerjee (TMC) → 68%

Strong TMC dominance here, especially after the 2021 by-election where Mamata Banerjee consolidated personal vote bank. BJP has urban pockets but lacks consistent booth-level strength. Left presence is now mostly symbolic.

<H3>Insight
* Bhabanipur has been a stronghold of TMC leadership figures for over a decade.
* Shift from Congress vs TMC (2006–2011) to TMC vs BJP (post-2016) reflects state-wide political realignment.
* Mamata Banerjee’s personal credibility and symbolic importance make this seat less about party and more about leadership.
