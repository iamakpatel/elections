# Kolkata District

## Bhabanipur Constituency

## 2026 Candidates & Winning Chances
- Mamata Banerjee (TMC) → 62%
- Priyanka Tibrewal (BJP) → 30%
- CPI(M) Candidate → 8%

> Mamata Banerjee has consistently dominated Bhabanipur, especially after the 2021 bypoll win with a massive margin. Strong grassroots network, minority vote consolidation, and urban TMC loyalty make her the clear favorite. BJP retains a solid urban vote base but struggles to breach TMC’s dominance here.

### Insight
- Bhabanipur transitioned from a Congress stronghold (2006) to a TMC fortress post-2011.
- Mamata Banerjee’s personal influence turned this into a prestige seat.
- BJP emerged as the main challenger after 2016 but hasn’t converted vote share into a serious winning threat.
- Urban middle-class + minority vote consolidation is the key reason for TMC dominance.# Kolkata
